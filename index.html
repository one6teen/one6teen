OUR STORY GET INVOLVED 64.5% of SA children grow up without their father 62.4% youth aged 15-24 are unemployed 1 in 3 young people will never have a meaningful mentor 18.8% of children grow up with neither parent present WHO WE ARE UNASHAMED. Romans 1:16. One6Teen Foundation (116) is a registered South African Non-Profit Company built on a single conviction: a generation without formation is a generation without direction. We exist to restore intentional mentorship and relational leadership in the lives of young people ages 13-25. We step into the gaps left by absent fathers and missing role models, equipping youth through faith-informed mentorship, leadership development, creative expression, and sport. We are not building a programme. We are building a movement. And movements need people willing to show up consistently for the generation that needs them most. VISION A generation formed, guided, and empowered to rise as confident leaders who transform their communities, society, and world into places of hope, opportunity, and lasting impact. MISSION To restore intentional mentorship and relational leadership in the lives of young people by stepping into the gaps left by absent fathers and role models. TAGLINE UNASHAMED. CASCADING MENTORSHIP MODEL Pass It Down. No one is too young to lead. No one is too experienced to still need guidance. Everyone in the chain is simultaneously being poured into and pouring out. TIER 1 University Mentors University students and young adults Close enough in age to be relatable. Far enough ahead to be relevant. University students step back into high schools as peer mentors bringing lived experience and a presence no adult can replicate. TIER 2 Senior Learners Grade 10, 11, 12 mentoring Grade 8, 9 Within the school, senior learners are trained and deployed as peer mentors. Same corridors. Same culture. Intentional leadership activated, structured, and consistent. TIER 3 The Pipeline Junior learners becoming future mentors Every young person being mentored today is a future mentor. As they grow, they move up the chain. The movement multiplies through people, not budget. WHY IT WORKS: Peer influence is one of the most powerful forces in a young person's life. Pass It Down does not fight that force. It redirects it. ANNUAL FLAGSHIP GATHERING The Formation Camp. Once a year. Every generation. Something permanent. The Formation Camp is One6Teen's annual gathering. For three days, youth, fathers, and mentors step out of their ordinary environments and into something intentional. It is not a programme. It is an encounter where walls come down, where fathers speak honestly to their children, and where young people discover something about themselves they could not find anywhere else. "Three days where fathers show up, mentors invest, and young people discover who they were always meant to be." Generations Together Youth, dads, and mentors in the same space, around the same fire, in the same movement. The generation gap closes here. Presence Over Programme Every activity exists to create genuine human presence. Adults who are fully there. Young people who feel fully seen. Identity Formed Three days of intentional formation. Being asked the questions that matter, being challenged, being celebrated. Sent Out With Purpose The camp ends with a commissioning. Every person leaves with purpose and an accountability partner to carry it with them. PERFORMANCE ARTS PLATFORM The 116 Collective. One6Teen's developing community of performance artists, singers, musicians, and creatives being intentionally mentored, trained, and prepared for real platforms. This is not entertainment for its own sake. It is formation through exposure, and exposure through excellence. "If you have a stage, we have artists who are ready for it." The 116 Collective performs at corporate events, embassy functions, gala dinners, and high-profile platforms. Every performance is formation through the discipline of craft and the experience of a real stage. -> Corporate Events and Gala Dinners -> Embassy and Government Functions -> Awards Ceremonies -> Community and Church Events -> High-Profile Public Platforms BOOK THE COLLECTIVE JOIN THE MOVEMENT There is a role for everyone.
